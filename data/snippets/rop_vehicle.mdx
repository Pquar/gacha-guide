# Mobile Fortress

System that greatly increases account POWER and STATUS (ATK, DEF, HP, SPD...).

In this system, you have two main strategies for leveling up vehicles:
either upgrading them evenly to maximize overall power gain,
or focusing on hitting specific milestones (150, 100, 150) to unlock the [Essence Restoration] bonus as quickly as possible — which is the recommended approach.

In [Evolve], you will always start by progressing through the SPD track, followed by ATK → HP → DEF.

[Dismantle] is not worth using early on, similar to Guardians; you should only dismantle once your Steles are fully maxed.

Summons in this system are worth purchasing, as they provide SHARDs along with powerful bonuses.

If an event appears offering tools to speed up vehicle leveling, it is generally not worth investing in,
since this system does not grant SPD boosts through evolution — only [Essence Restoration] bonuses.

Typically, it takes about 10 to 15 days to obtain a red vehicle if you do not purchase any additional packs.

Essence Restoration: gain on hitting specific milestones (50, 100, 150) and STARS upgrading.
